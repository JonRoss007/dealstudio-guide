About the System

What is the Trent Lee System?

The Trent Lee System is a premium, done-for-you automation package available inside Deal Studio. It was developed by Trent Lee of First Choice Business Brokers, one of the top-producing business brokers in the United States.

🎯
In plain terms: It gives your brokerage a fully scripted communication system so that every buyer and seller receives a professional, high-frequency follow-up experience — without your team writing a single word or sending a single manual message.

Automation Logic

How It Works Inside Deal Studio

The Trent Lee System runs on top of your existing GoHighLevel (GHL) CRM. When a contact moves to a pipeline stage, the correct sequence fires automatically.

TriggerWhat Fires Automatically
Contact enters Inquiry ReceivedNDA email + SMS sequence with intro video from Trent's assistant
NDA is signed electronicallyThank-you SMS + CIM / blind profile delivery sequence
Buyer/Seller meeting is scheduledPrep email + SMS + calendar reminder with prep video
Meeting is missed3-email reschedule sequence fires automatically
Meeting is completedPost-meeting follow-up: email → wait → task → email → wait → email
Contact stalls in any stageCRM task auto-created to prompt broker to check in manually
Seller listing goes Active (On Market)Listing confirmation email + financial update request every 70 days
Listing is soldPost-closing congratulations + gift request + Google review ask
💡
Key difference from standard automations: It is not just volume. It is the quality, timing, tone, and use of video at every major stage — designed to build trust and keep deals moving without manual effort.

Setup Process

Moving from Standard to Trent Lee

If you are a current Deal Studio client considering the upgrade, the process is handled by your Deal Studio Implementation Specialist. Here is a summary of what is involved.

⚠️
Before activation: Videos, calendar links, and SMS numbers need to be configured per broker. Your Implementation Specialist will walk through each of these during the setup process before going live.
1
Trent Lee package installed
The full workflow library is imported into your GHL sub-account by your Deal Studio Implementation Specialist.
2
Video placeholders replaced
Each sequence includes video thumbnails. Your team updates these to your own Vimeo or YouTube videos, or keeps Trent Lee videos as licensed content.
3
Calendar links configured
Booking links in emails are updated to point to each broker's own calendar. This is done per broker before any workflows go live.
4
SMS and voicemail set up
The SMS sending number and voicemail recordings are configured in GHL before the workflows are activated.
5
Standard automations reviewed
Existing standard sequences are reviewed to avoid duplication or conflict with the new Trent Lee workflows.
6
Testing and go-live
Workflows are tested on dummy contacts before being activated for live use across the brokerage.
Fit Assessment

Is It Right for You?

Both systems serve different needs. Use this to guide the conversation with clients who are evaluating the upgrade.

✅Trent Lee is a great fit if you...
  • ✓Are actively marketing to both buyers and sellers and want to reduce manual follow-up.
  • ✓Are onboarding new brokers who don't yet have their own communication templates.
  • ✓Want a proven, tested communication system rather than building sequences from scratch.
  • ✓Want to use video as a trust-building tool with prospects at every stage.
  • ✓Are losing deals due to inconsistent follow-up and need a system to hold it together.
🔹Standard is sufficient if you...
  • →Already have strong manual follow-up habits and mainly need the NDA flow and stage notifications.
  • →Are a smaller operation just getting started who wants to build familiarity with Deal Studio first.
  • →Prefer to write your own communication and only need the CRM infrastructure in place.
